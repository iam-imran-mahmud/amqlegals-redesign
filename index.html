Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I incorporate my business? +

AMQ Legals offers same-day incorporation, often completing Ontario business registration in as little as 30 minutes. Our priority filing service typically processes within 2 business hours.

What do I need to incorporate? +

You'll need a unique business name, the names of directors and shareholders (one person can fill all roles), and a registered office address. We handle everything else, including the NUANS report.

Are there residency requirements? +

Federal incorporation requires at least 25% Canadian resident directors. Many provinces have removed residency requirements. We'll guide you to the best jurisdiction for your situation.

What is a holding company? +

A holding company owns and manages investments (shares, real estate, IP) without conducting day-to-day operations. It offers asset protection, tax planning, and strategic control over subsidiaries.

What's the difference between for-profit and non-profit? +

For-profit businesses generate revenue for owners or shareholders. Non-profits are formed for social or community missions — they don't issue shares and reinvest any surplus toward their purpose.

Do you offer support after incorporation? +

Absolutely. We provide document organization, ongoing compliance filings (annual returns, notice of change), and business growth support — so you're never alone after you incorporate.
