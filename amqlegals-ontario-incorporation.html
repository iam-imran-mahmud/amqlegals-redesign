What You'll Need

Requirements to Incorporate in Ontario

01
Business Activities (NAICS Code)

A description of your primary business activities and the corresponding NAICS industry code. We'll help you identify the right code if you're unsure.

02
Director Names & Addresses

Full legal names and addresses for all directors. Worldwide addresses are accepted. At least one director must be 18+ years old. No Canadian residency required for Ontario corporations.

03
Registered Ontario Address

A physical address in Ontario is required as the registered office. PO boxes are not accepted. AMQ Legals offers an address & mail-forwarding service if needed.

04
Valid NUANS Report (Named Corps)

A NUANS name search report is mandatory for named corporations to confirm your business name is unique. This is included in all AMQ Legals packages. Numbered corporations skip this step.

05
Desired Incorporation Date

Choose today's date for immediate incorporation, or a future date if you need to plan your business launch. We'll file on schedule.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about incorporating in Ontario with AMQ Legals.

What is the process for incorporating in Ontario? +

Incorporating in Ontario involves choosing a business name, filing Articles of Incorporation with the Ontario Business Registry, and fulfilling legal and tax requirements. AMQ Legals handles this entire process — from name search to document delivery — ensuring full compliance with provincial regulations.

How much does it cost to incorporate in Ontario? +

AMQ Legals packages start at $300, with government filing fees included. There are no hidden charges. The price covers name search, articles preparation, legal review, and same-day document delivery.

How long does incorporation take with AMQ Legals? +

With our rush service, incorporation can be completed in as little as 30 minutes to 1 hour. Standard processing is typically completed the same business day. Timing depends on the type (numbered vs. named) and required government approvals.

Do I need a lawyer to incorporate in Ontario? +

While hiring a lawyer is not mandatory, having legal professionals review your documents helps prevent errors. AMQ Legals includes an in-house legal review with every package, ensuring your documents are fully compliant without the cost of hiring a private lawyer.

Can a non-resident incorporate a business in Ontario? +

Yes. Unlike federal incorporation, Ontario does not require any directors to be Canadian residents. Non-residents can fully incorporate an Ontario company. AMQ Legals specializes in helping international entrepreneurs set up Canadian businesses.

Federal vs. provincial incorporation — which is better? +

Provincial (Ontario) incorporation lets you operate primarily within the province at lower cost. Federal incorporation lets you operate across all of Canada under one registration, but requires at least 25% Canadian resident directors. AMQ Legals helps you choose the best option for your business goals.

Do I need a NUANS report to incorporate in Ontario? +

Yes, if you're registering a named corporation. The NUANS report confirms your business name is unique and available. It is included in all AMQ Legals packages. If you choose a numbered corporation, no name search is required.

What are the ongoing requirements after incorporation? +

After incorporation, you must file an Initial Return within 60 days, file Annual Returns each year, maintain a registered Ontario office address, and update director or officer changes with the registry. AMQ Legals offers ongoing compliance services to keep your corporation in good standing.
